Journal

EJ is electronic journal. If will track list of transactions that has happened on the atm. The dispensed bills from each cassette box, the card holder to whom cash was dispensed, the transaction sequence number and authorization code of the transaction. If will also log any errors that has occurred on the ATM. this journal will be helpful for reconciliation purpose. Meaning rechecking the dispense amount when cardholder is claiming that he has not got the cash Effortlessly retrieve your Electronic Journal files to quickly resolve customer disputes. The easy-to-use interface enables you to retrieve EJ files by using ATM ID and DATE. Electronic Journal Management uses a web-based technology to provide you with easy-to-use search options, including ATM IDs and EJ history on each ATM. NCR maintains a minimum of six months history.
